Please add your final resume PDF at this path: `cv/resume.pdf`.

You can generate it by:
- Editing `cv/resume.html` then printing to PDF from your browser and saving as `cv/resume.pdf`, OR
- Converting `cv/resume-template.md` to PDF using pandoc (see `cv/README.md`).

Once `cv/resume.pdf` exists, the "Download Resume" button on the site will download the file.
